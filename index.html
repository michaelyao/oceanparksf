Peace and Quiet in the Heart of the City

Ocean Park is San Francisco’s premier coastal area: the San Francisco Zoo is your backyard; Lake Merced is your private pool; several golf courses are at your beck and call; the Pacific Ocean’s deep blue sea meets the azure sky; and the weather is excellent at an average annual temperature of 68oF (20oC).

Ocean Park offers 1500-square-feet ocean-view condominiums with southern exposure, spacious balconies, and 360-degree landscape views. Relax and enjoy the panoramic beach and ocean views from your spacious balcony, a new and perfect oceanfront living experience.

Ocean View

Relax at home, admire the blue ocean and azure sky, caress the blue sea breeze, and fall in love anew with each romantic sunset.

Lifestyle

Breathe fresh air filled with the scent of sunshine carried on cool ocean breeze. Enjoy the breathtaking panoramic skyline through floor-to-ceiling windows.

Close to Nature

The San Francisco Zoo is home to over 1,000 exotic animals representing more than 250 species, as well as peaceful gardens planted with both native and foreign plants. Your children can explore a wonderful animal kingdom and experience an amazing forest environment just across the street.

Beach Life

Stretching over three miles in length, San Francisco’s Ocean Beach is a major attraction for surfers and beachcombers the world over. If you enjoy spending time outdoors and escaping the crowds, Ocean Beach is the perfect destination, and it can be found 1 minutes away.

Top Schools

Beautiful Ocean Park is situated in one of the California's best school districts for kindergarten through high school. Lowell High School is ranked #3 in all of California.

Commute

Walking distance to Ocean Beach and across the street from the San Francisco Zoo. Immediate access to bus numbers 18, 22, and 48. A short ride on the L-Taraval line leads directly to downtown San Francisco.
